# Platrare Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026  

This policy describes how the **Platrare** mobile app (“the app”) handles information. Platrare is built as a **privacy-first, local-first** finance tracker: your ledger stays on your device unless **you** choose to export it.

## Who we are

Platrare is provided by the developer/publisher listed on the App Store or Google Play listing. This policy uses “we” to mean that publisher. **We do not operate a cloud service for your financial data** and we do not receive your accounts, transactions, or balances on our servers through normal use of the app.

## What data the app stores (on your device)

- **Financial ledger**: accounts, transactions, planned transactions, categories, and optional **file attachments** (e.g. photos of receipts) you add.
- **Settings**: preferences such as base/secondary currency, theme, language, optional app lock (PIN / device biometrics), and similar options stored locally.
- **Exchange rates cache**: downloaded public currency rates used for display and conversion, cached on device.

Data is stored **locally** on your phone or tablet (e.g. application documents / SQLite). **We do not sync this data to our servers** because the app does not use a Platrare account or backend for your ledger.

## Data you send over the internet

- **Exchange rates**: The app may request **anonymous** exchange-rate data from a public HTTP API (e.g. Frankfurter) so it can convert amounts. These requests **do not include** your account names, transaction details, or balances.
- **No analytics SDK**: The app is not intended to send your financial content to third-party analytics or advertising networks as part of its core design. (If you later add such tools, update this policy and the store listing accordingly.)

## Optional app lock

If you enable **Lock app on open**, the app uses **device biometrics** and/or a **PIN** you set. Biometric and secure-enclave behavior is provided by **iOS / Android**, not by us. A PIN may be stored as a **cryptographic hash** locally (not as plain text) for verification.

## Backups (export / import)

You can **export** a backup file (`.zip` or password-protected `.platrare`) to a location **you** choose (Files, cloud folder, AirDrop, etc.). **You control that copy.** We do not host or receive your backup file.

**Import** replaces the current on-device database with the backup contents. Use imports only from backups you trust.

## Photos and files

If you attach images or files to transactions, the app uses the **photo library** and/or **camera** as described in the system permission prompts. That media stays with your local data unless you export a backup that includes attachments.

## Data retention and deletion

- **On device**: Data remains until you delete it in the app, import a backup that overwrites it, or **uninstall** the app (which removes app-local storage subject to OS behavior).
- **Portability**: Use **Export** to obtain a copy of your ledger in the backup format.
- **No “account deletion” with us**: There is no Platrare login; erasing data is done on your device (and by removing backups you stored elsewhere).

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). Do not use it for child-directed services without appropriate consent.

## Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

Use the support contact given on the App Store / Google Play listing (or in-app **Copy support info** if available) for privacy questions.
